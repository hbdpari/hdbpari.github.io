When I was in the darkest pit of hell,
You were there to pull me.
If I could revert time,
There is no doubt i would search for you sooner
This is the day that
Brought you to me.
This is the day that
Made everything we did possible
Happy Birthday to you
Hope you will always be at serenity
and in the darkest of days
Hope you will find peace within yourself.
